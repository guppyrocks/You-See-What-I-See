How did I started getting into Photography?

I first started finding interest in photography a little after graduating highschool. I have always used this social network called Tumblr and followed a bunch of creatives over in the west coast; California based. Everyday, scrolling down my dashboard, I would see a bunch of dope photography and it has always been in the back on my mind. How did they do this? it changed my whole perspective on how I started viewing things. Around this time, iPhone cameras were developing pretty good enough to take pictures with, my friends started telling me they like my pictures and my brother had a camera laying around.. I picked it up and the rest was history. My style of photography branches off streetwear, lifestyles, to portraits. I shoot with my film camera; it's a 1983 Nikon L35AF point and shoot and with my digital Nikon D5000. My style of photography branches off from streetwear, lifestyles, to portraits.
